Biography

I'm Fredrick Onyango, a Health Informatics researcher and data scientist passionate about using technology to solve real-world health challenges. My work sits at the intersection of clinical data, machine learning, and public health analytics.

Currently focused on building predictive models for disease detection, designing data-driven dashboards for emergency response, and analyzing social determinants of health through spatial data science. I believe data, when used thoughtfully, has the power to save lives.

Based in Indianapolis, Indiana — working with institutions like the Polis Center on urban health analytics and equity-driven research.
