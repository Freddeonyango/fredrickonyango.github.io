AI in Healthcare Ethics

October 28, 2025

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is changing the way we think about healthcare. From predicting disease risks to guiding treatment decisions, it promises faster, smarter, and more personalized care. But as someone passionate about health informatics and data science, I believe we must carefully consider the ethical implications before fully relying on AI in clinical settings. Three areas stand out to me: decision-making, privacy, and patient safety.

First, clinical decision-making. AI can process enormous amounts of patient data, detect patterns that humans might miss, and provide recommendations in seconds. Finally, patient safety cannot be overlooked. AI models are only as good as the data they are trained on, and biased data can lead to biased outcomes.
